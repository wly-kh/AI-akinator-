KAU AI Chatbot: A Deductive Digital Assistant

The King Abdulaziz University (KAU) AI Chatbot project is an innovative digital solution designed to transform the way students and visitors access campus information. Drawing inspiration from the interactive and deductive logic popularized by Akinator, this chatbot utilizes advanced artificial intelligence to pinpoint the specific information a user requires through a series of intuitive, clarifying questions. This methodology allows the system to efficiently navigate the complex web of KAU’s academic departments, administrative services, and campus regulations, providing a tailored and engaging user experience that goes beyond standard search engines.

This AI-powered platform serves as a comprehensive guide for the KAU community, addressing a wide spectrum of inquiries ranging from admission requirements and course registration to locating specific facilities across the university's expansive grounds. By leveraging modern machine learning techniques, the chatbot ensures that information is accessible 24/7, reducing the administrative burden on university staff and empowering students with immediate, accurate data. The project represents a significant step forward in integrating practical AI tools within the educational ecosystem of King Abdulaziz University, creating a more connected and technologically advanced campus environment for all.
