description 
The King Abdulaziz University (KAU) AI Chatbot is an intelligent digital assistant that utilizes deductive logic, similar to the Akinator concept, to provide information.
The project leverages artificial intelligence to help students and visitors find accurate answers to their inquiries regarding academic programs, administrative services, and university facilities through a smooth, interactive conversation.

The bot aims to simplify information access within the campus and provide a 24/7 digital guide, enhancing technical efficiency and offering an organized user experience for the KAU community.
 It covers a wide range of inquiries, from admission and registration procedures to specific details about campus locations and services.
